Declaration

The hostel food menu is prepared on a weekly basis by the mess management. Students can suggest changes based on their preferences. The menu may be modified accordingly and updated regularly.

Mess Committee & Meeting

A mess committee is formed with student representatives and hostel management. Meetings are conducted regularly to discuss food quality and menu improvements. Students can provide feedback and request changes. Final decisions are approved by hostel authorities.
